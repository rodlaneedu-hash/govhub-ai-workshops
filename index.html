AI in Schools Workshop Series

Resources and recordings for this workshop series are emailed directly to registered participants.

If you are registered for a workshop, you will receive your resources the day before the session and the recording the day after. To register, visit the GovHub AI Services page at tewhakaroputanga.org.nz/our-services-2/ai-services.

If you have not received your resources, please contact the workshop organiser.
